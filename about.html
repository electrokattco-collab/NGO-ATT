The Story
Behind The Impact.

Tsoga African Child is a registered Non-Profit Company dedicated to addressing trauma and mental wellness among South African school learners through comprehensive, school-based intervention programs.

Our Genesis

Bridging the Gap Between Learning and Wellness.

The well-being of our children determines the future of our nation. Across South Africa, thousands of learners walk into classrooms each day carrying invisible burdens—trauma from abuse, neglect, poverty, and violence. These challenges manifest as behavioural problems, poor academic performance, and emotional distress.

Despite existing support structures within the education system, a disturbing pattern persists. Too many learners slip through the cracks, their struggles unnoticed or inadequately addressed. Traditional reactive approaches wait for crises to escalate before intervention.

Tsoga African Child recognizes that transformation requires a model shift—from reactive crisis management to proactive, sustained intervention. Our Supporting Resilience Mental Wellness (SRMW) initiative represents a comprehensive, long-term commitment to identifying, supporting, and nurturing learners who face social and emotional challenges.

Our Vision

To transform classrooms and school environments across South Africa into safe spaces of joy, healing, and comfort where every learner, regardless of their circumstances, can thrive emotionally, socially, and academically.

Our Mission

To provide sustained, evidence-based intervention programs that support the holistic well-being of learners through comprehensive counselling services, educator empowerment, and family engagement, ultimately improving learner behaviour, academic performance, and life outcomes over an extended period of eight years or more.
