FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can a school partner with Tsoga African Child?

Simply fill out the contact form or email us at tsogaafricanchild@gmail.com. Our team will arrange an introductory meeting to discuss how the SRMW program can be implemented at your school.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, Tsoga African Child NPC is a registered Non-Profit Company (2024/659725/08) with SARS tax number 9641744199. We can provide Section 18A tax certificates for eligible donations upon request.

Do you offer individual counselling for learners?

Yes, our SRMW program includes weekly individual counselling sessions for identified learners. Referrals can be made by educators, parents, or through our classroom-based screening process.

How can I volunteer my skills?

Visit our Volunteer page and complete the application form. We review applications within 5-7 business days. We're particularly looking for counsellors, social workers, educator trainers, and administrative support.

What areas do you currently serve?

We are currently focused on schools in Atteridgeville, Mamelodi, and surrounding communities in Pretoria, Gauteng. We have plans to expand to other provinces as funding and capacity allow.

How long is the SRMW program commitment?

The SRMW initiative is designed as a long-term (8+ years) school-based program. This sustained approach allows us to create lasting change and truly transform school communities.
