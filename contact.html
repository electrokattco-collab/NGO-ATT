Intelligence Repository

How can a school partner with ATT NGO?

Simply fill out the contact form or email our partnerships team at partnerships@att-ngo.org.za and we will arrange an introductory presentation.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered NPO/PBO and can provide section 18A tax certificates for all donations above R500.

Do you offer individual counselling?

Our primary focus is group interventions and community workshops, but we do provide referrals to partner clinics for individual clinical cases.

How can I volunteer my skills?

Visit our Volunteer page and complete the Operational Intake Form. We review applications within 5-7 business days.
