The Challenge

A Quiet Crisis.
A Strategic Response.

Across South Africa, thousands of learners walk into classrooms each day carrying invisible burdens—trauma from abuse, neglect, poverty, and violence. These challenges manifest as behavioural problems, poor academic performance, and emotional distress.

Despite existing support structures, too many learners slip through the cracks. Traditional reactive approaches wait for crises to escalate before intervention. Tsoga African Child recognizes that transformation requires a model shift—from reactive crisis management to proactive, sustained intervention.
