Volunteer Information

What to Expect

Time Commitment

Flexible arrangements available. Most volunteers contribute 4-8 hours per week, with options for both short-term project work and long-term engagement.

Support Provided

All volunteers receive orientation, ongoing supervision, and access to professional development opportunities. Counsellors receive weekly clinical supervision.

Working Environment

Work directly in schools within Atteridgeville, Mamelodi, and surrounding communities. Our office is located at 7 Ramskin, Atteridgeville, Pretoria.

Recognition

Volunteers receive certificates of appreciation, references for future employment, and invitations to annual recognition events. Your contribution makes a real difference.
